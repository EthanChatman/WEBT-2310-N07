Joker

Joker, the protagonist of Persona 5 Royal, is a high school student who becomes the leader of the Phantom Thieves. Though quiet and reserved at first glance, he possesses a strong sense of justice and an unshakable will to fight against corruption. His calm demeanor and sharp intellect make him a natural strategist, while his ability to wield multiple Personas reflects his adaptability and open-mindedness. Joker’s journey is about balancing his everyday student life with the responsibilities of leading a team that challenges the moral failings of society.

Violet

Violet is a graceful and determined newcomer to the story, known for her elegance and athletic talent. She exudes a cheerful, optimistic personality and often displays great respect for others. Beneath her poise, however, lies a deep desire to improve herself and live up to her own ideals. Violet’s relationship with the protagonist highlights themes of personal growth, confidence, and self-discovery, making her a vital emotional presence within the game’s expanded narrative.

Crow

Crow is a charismatic and ambitious individual who brings a mix of charm and intellect to the Phantom Thieves. He takes pride in his abilities and approaches challenges with a confident, sometimes competitive spirit. Though he often presents himself as composed and sophisticated, Crow also wrestles with questions about justice, recognition, and the weight of expectations placed upon him. His character adds complexity and tension to the group dynamic, offering a distinct perspective on what it means to be a hero.

Maruki Takuto

Dr. Maruki serves as a compassionate school counselor introduced in Persona 5 Royal. He deeply cares about the well-being of students and strives to understand their emotional struggles. His calm and empathetic nature makes him a trusted figure, offering support and insight to those who feel lost or burdened. Maruki’s role in the story expands the game’s exploration of mental health, happiness, and the human desire to find peace amid hardship, giving him a quiet yet profound significance in the overall narrative.
