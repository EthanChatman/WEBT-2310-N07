About The Cookie Mart

Small-batch cookies, big flavors — baked daily and shared with care.

Welcome to The Cookie Mart. We’re a neighborhood bakery and online shop dedicated to creating handcrafted cookies using high-quality ingredients and thoughtful recipes. Our focus is simple: bake delicious cookies that bring people together.

Who We Are

The Cookie Mart began as a small weekend stall in 2018 and grew from local demand for better cookies. Our team includes passionate bakers, recipe developers, and customer service folks who care about every detail — from ingredient sourcing to packaging. We bake in small batches to ensure freshness and consistency.

Our Mission

We aim to make everyday moments sweeter by delivering thoughtfully made cookies. Sustainability, quality, and community are at the heart of what we do: we reduce waste where we can, choose better ingredients, and support local producers.

What We Do

  • Handcrafted Cookies: Classic and seasonal flavors, baked fresh daily in small batches.
  • Online Orders & Local Delivery: Easy online ordering and fast local delivery where available.
  • Wholesale & Events: Custom assortments for cafes, corporate events, and celebrations.
  • Gift Boxes & Subscriptions: Curated gift boxes and limited subscription drops for frequent customers.
