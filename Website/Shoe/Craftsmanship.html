Craftsmanship & Services

At SoleCraft we focus on construction techniques and customer services that keep your shoes comfortable and long-lasting.

Construction Methods

Goodyear Welt: Durable, resoleable construction used on many of our dress and boot designs.
Stitched Cup Sole: Lightweight and flexible for sneakers and casual styles.
Vegan Bonding: A strong, non-leather option for EcoStep pieces.

Fit & Comfort

We design footbeds with arch support, breathable linings, and cushioned midsoles. Try our in-store fitting service for the best size and width recommendations.

Repair Program

Keep your favorite pair in rotation — we offer resoling, heel repairs, and leather conditioning. Repairs extend product life and reduce the need for replacement.

Custom Orders

Ask about semi-custom color combinations and sizing adjustments for selected styles. Small-batch customization is available by request.

Quick Service Facts

  • Resoling turnaround: 2–3 weeks
  • Custom consultations by appointment
  • Complimentary clean & condition with repairs
